Courses

OSWINDS team supports graduate courses in the Department’s Master of science programs in Informatics and Communications, the “Internet and World Wide Web” and in the cross-thematic Master on “Informatics and Management”.
All of graduate courses focus on the areas of Web information systems and place emphasis on Web science principles, methodologies and emerging technologies, with project work targeting both research and experimentation on Web technologies. The next courses are offered with more information on the corresponding official Departments graduate programs sites.
• Course “Web information mining and retrieval”, 2nd semester of MSc studies on “Informatics and Communications”, at the direction of study on “Data, knowledge and software technologies”. More details
• Course “Social Media Analysis”, of 2nd semester of MSc studies on “Internet and World Wide Web” program. More details
• Course “Web information systems“, 1ST semester of MSc studies on “Informatics and Management”. More details
